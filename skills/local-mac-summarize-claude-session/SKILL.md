## Capture Session Summary

This skill captures your session thinking, decisions, and learnings to your Obsidian vault.

**How it works:**

1. Read your vault's CLAUDE.md at `~/Documents/claude_documents/CLAUDE.md`
2. Understand your vault's structure, conventions, and session capture guidelines
3. Create a comprehensive session summary following vault principles
4. Save to Daily folder with today's date

**That's it.** Your vault CLAUDE.md is the source of truth for:
- How sessions should be structured
- What sections to include
- How to link to projects and concepts
- How to integrate with your knowledge graph
- Conventions for your extended intelligence system

Claude will read your vault's instructions and know what to do.
