# LIFE_OS Wiki

**Full documentation:** Vault → `LIFE_OS/INDEX.md`

## Overview

LIFE_OS is a personal operating system layer in the vault — a structured approach to managing personal domains (health, finance, goals, thinking frameworks, contacts) with the same rigor as work projects.

## Domains

| Domain | Purpose |
|--------|---------|
| Health | Physical and mental wellbeing — routines, habits, energy |
| Finance | Personal financial planning and tracking |
| Thinking | Mental models, frameworks, and reflective practices |
| Goals | Long-term goals and life direction |
| Contacts | Personal address book and relationships |

## Key Concept

LIFE_OS separates personal material from work projects and market analysis — keeping both layers explicit and navigable without noise.
